# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free environment for all contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Focus on facts and actionable feedback

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or abusive language
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions and discussions that violate this code.
